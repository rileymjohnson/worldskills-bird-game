How to Play

  • Move the airplane using the arrows keys
  • The timer present the time lapsed
  • The fuel counter show the remain fuel
  • During the flight, the aircraft need to avoid the birds that are presented in the sky. If the airplane hit a bird, the game is over
  • During the flight, the aircraft need to collect fuel and stars in the sky
  • You can pause the game clicking in a button pause, or pressing the space bar.
  • Go beyond all limits
